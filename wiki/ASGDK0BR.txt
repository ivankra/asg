When someone asks you "what on earth are you doing?," "究竟 (jiūjìng)" is used by the speaker to intensify the question. In this case, 究竟 is similar to [[Expressing "In the End" with "daodi"|到底]], but a bit more formal.

== Using 究竟 in Questions ==

=== Using 究竟 in Targeted Questions===

These questions are aimed at finding out a specific piece of info, such as a name or time.

==== Structure ====

<div class="jiegou">

究竟 + Question Word

</div>

==== Examples ====

<div class="liju">   

*这 个 人 <em>究竟</em> 是 谁？<span class="pinyin">Zhège rén <em>jiūjìng</em> shì shéi?</span><span class="trans">Who on earth is this guy?</span>
*你 <em>究竟</em> 是 做 什么 的？<span class="pinyin">Nǐ <em>jiūjìng</em> shì zuò shénme de?</span><span class="trans">What exactly is it that you do?</span>
*他们 <em>究竟</em> 什么 时候 到？<span class="pinyin">Tāmen <em>jiūjìng</em> shénme shíhou dào?</span><span class="trans">When exactly are they arriving?</span>
*我们 <em>究竟</em> 还 要 等 多久？<span class="pinyin">Wǒmen <em>jiūjìng</em> hái yào děng duōjiǔ?</span><span class="trans">How much longer do we still need to wait?</span>
*你们 <em>究竟</em> 想 让 我 怎么 做？<span class="pinyin">Nǐmen <em>jiūjìng</em> xiǎng ràng wǒ zěnme zuò?</span><span class="trans">What on earth do you want me to do?</span>	

</div>

=== Using 究竟 in Yes/No Questions ===

==== Structure ====

<div class="jiegou">

究竟 ＋ Verb ＋ 没 / 不 ＋ Verb

</div>

==== Examples ====

<div class="liju">   

*你 <em>究竟</em> 来 不 来？<span class="pinyin">Nǐ <em>jiūjìng</em> lái bu lái?</span><span class="trans">Are you coming or not?</span>
*你们 <em>究竟</em>  能 不 能 帮 我？<span class="pinyin">Nǐmen <em>jiūjìng</em> néng bu néng bāng wǒ?</span><span class="trans">Can you guys help me or not?</span>
*她 <em>究竟</em> 是 不 是 你 女朋友？<span class="pinyin">Tā <em>jiūjìng</em> shì bu shì nǐ nǚpéngyou?</span><span class="trans">So, is she your girlfriend or not?</span>
*你 <em>究竟</em> 喜 不 喜欢 我？<span class="pinyin">Nǐ <em>jiūjìng</em> xǐ bu xǐhuan wǒ?</span><span class="trans">So, do you like me or not?</span>
*经理 <em>究竟</em> 同 不 同意？<span class="pinyin">Jīnglǐ <em>jiūjìng</em> tóng bu tóngyì?</span><span class="trans">Did the manager agree or not?</span>

</div>

== Using 究竟 in Affirmative Sentences ==

究竟 can also be used with 是 for an emphasis of a fact, which is similar to [[Expressing "after all" with "bijing"|毕竟]], meaning "after all." 
 
=== Structure ===

<div class="jiegou">

Fact ＋ 究竟 + 是 ＋ Fact，……

</div>

=== Examples === 

<div class="liju">   

*孩子 <em>究竟</em> 是 孩子，总是 想 着 怎么 玩。<span class="pinyin">Háizi <em>jiūjìng</em> shì háizi, zǒng shì xiǎng zhe zěnme wán.</span><span class="trans">Kids are kids after all, always thinking about how to have fun.</span>	
*上海 <em>究竟</em> 是 上海，生活 成本 就是 高。<span class="pinyin">Shànghǎi <em>jiūjìng</em> shì Shànghǎi, shēnghuó chéngběn jiùshì gāo.</span><span class="trans">Shanghai is Shanghai after all. The living expenses here are just high.</span>
*年轻 人 <em>究竟</em> 是 年轻 人，精力 真 好。<span class="pinyin">Niánqīng rén <em>jiūjìng</em> shì niánqīng rén, jīnglì zhēn hǎo.</span><span class="trans">Young people are young, after all. They are so full of energy.</span>	
*生意人 <em>究竟</em> 是 生意人，对 他们 来说，赚钱 是 最 重要 的 。<span class="pinyin">Shēngyìrén <em>jiūjìng</em> shì shēngyìrén, duì tāmen lái shuō, zhuànqián shì zuì zhòngyào de.</span><span class="trans">Businessmen are businessmen, after all. The most important thing to them is making money.</span>	
*家人 <em>究竟</em> 是 家人，也许 你 不 喜欢 他们，但 你 还是 会 爱 他们。<span class="pinyin">Jiārén <em>jiūjìng</em> shì jiārén, yěxǔ nǐ bù xǐhuan tāmen, dàn nǐ háishì huì ài tāmen.</span><span class="trans">Family is family, after all. You may not like them, but you still love them.</span>	

</div>
