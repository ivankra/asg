从而 roughly translates to "thus" or "thereby". It is used to express the consequent results of an action. 
== Structure ==

<div class="jiegou">
⋯⋯ ， 从而 ⋯⋯
</div>

== Examples ==

<div class="liju">

*不断 挖掘 潜在 客户 ， 扩大 客户 群 ，<em>从而</em> 迅速 占领 市场 。<span class="pinyin">Bùduàn wājué qiánzài kèhù, kuòdà kèhù qún, <em>cóng'ér</em> xùnsù zhànlǐng shìchǎng.</span><span class="trans">By continuously uncovering potential customers and expanding the client base, you thereby quickly occupy the market.</span>
*公司 要 加大 宣传 力度 ，<em>从而</em> 提高 品牌 知名度 。<span class="pinyin">Gōngsī yào jiā dà xuānchuán lìdù, <em>cóng'ér</em> tígāo pǐnpái zhīmíngdù.</span><span class="trans">The company should increase publicity to improve brand awareness.</span>
*他查找了很多资料，也问了很多人，<em>从而</em>解决了这个问题 。<span class="trans">Tā cházhǎo le hěn duō zīliào, yě wèn le hěn duō rén, cóngér jiějué le zhège wèntí.</span>
*这样做我们可以更快地完成这个项目，<em>从而</em>节省时间。<span class="trans">Zhèyàng zuò wǒmen kěyǐ gèng kuài de wánchéng zhège xiàngmù cóngér jiéshěng shíjiān.</span>
*这次他控制住了自己的脾气，<em>从而</em>避免了一场争斗。<span class="trans">Zhè cì tā kòngzhì zhù le zìjǐ de píqi, cóngér bìmiǎn le yī cháng zhēngdòu.</span>

</div>
