副词，用于反问，"为什么一定要⋯⋯"的意思，表示"不必"，语气"不必"重。多用于后一分句。

== Used before Predicate ==

=== Structure ===

In this case, 何必 is often used to indicate the speaker's attitude about something unnecessary or not worth doing.

<div class="jiegou">
Subj. + 何必 + Predicate 
</div>

=== Examples ===

<div class="liju">

* 他 已经 道歉 了 ，你 <em>何必</em> 还 生气 ？<span class="trans">.</span>
* 都是 一家人 ，你们 <em>何必</em> 这么 客气 ？<span class="trans">.</span>
* 上海 的 房子 那么 贵 ，你儿子 <em>何必</em> 要 在 那儿 买 房子 ？<span class="trans">.</span>
* 我 只是 不小心 踩 了 你 一下  ，你 <em>何必</em> 骂人 ？<span class="trans">.</span>

</div>

口语中，谓语可省略主要动词，"何必"直接加名词性成分。如：

<div class="liju">
*<em>何必</em> 下周 ？这 周 我 就 有 时间 。<span class="trans">Why next week?(Why wait?) I have time this week.</span>
*<em>何必</em> 总经理 ？经理 也 可以 做 代表 。<span class="trans">Why the general manager? The manager can totally be our representative.</span>
</div>

== Used before Another Clause ==

=== Structure ===

<div class="jiegou">
何必⋯⋯ 呢 ？⋯⋯ 
</div>

=== Examples ===

<div class="liju">

* <em>何必</em> 大家 都 去 呢 ？一个人 去 就 行了 。<span class="trans">?</span>
* <em>何必</em> 亲自 过来 呢 ？打个电话 就 行了 。<span class="trans">.</span>
* <em>何必</em> 麻烦 别人 呢 ？我 自己 能 行 。<span class="trans">.</span>

</div>

== Used as Predicate ==

何必 can be used alone with 呢 together to mean "why?"

A few examples:

<div class="liju">
* 你 又 买 了 礼物 ？<em>何必</em> 呢 ？<span class="trans">You bought us gifts again? Why?</span>
* 为了 买 张 电影票 排 那么 长 时间 的 队 ，<em>何必</em> 呢 ？<span class="trans">Spend so much time on waiting to buy a movie ticket? Why?</span>
* 为 这样 的 人 伤心 ，<em>何必</em> 呢 ？不 值得 。<span class="trans">Don't have to be so sad about this type of person. It's not worth it.</span>
</div>

== Colloquial Saying ==

<div class="liju">
* 早知如此，<em>何必</em> 当初 ？<span class="trans">If you had known it, why would you have done something like earlier?</span>
</div>
