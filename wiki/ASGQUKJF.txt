Use 岁 (suì) to give a person's age, similar to how we say "years old" in English. There are a details that work differently from English, however.

== Basic Structure for 岁 (suì) ==

=== Structure ===

The structure for telling someone's age with 岁 (suì) is:

<div class="jiegou">

Subj. + Number + 岁

</div>

This is equivalent to someone "'''is''' x years old" in English. Notice that you don't need to include any verb when you use 岁 (suì).

=== Examples ===

<div class="liju">

* 我 20 <em>岁</em> 。<span class="pinyin">Wǒ èrshí <em>suì</em>.</span><span class="trans">I am 20 years old.</span>
* 我 儿子 一 <em>岁</em> 。<span class="pinyin">Wǒ érzi yī <em>suì</em>.</span><span class="trans">My son is one year old.</span>
* 我 妈妈 今年 45 <em>岁</em> 。<span class="pinyin">Wǒ māma jīnnián sìshí-wǔ <em>suì</em>.</span><span class="trans">My mother is 45 years old this year.</span>
* 你 爷爷 今年 80 <em>岁</em> 吗？<span class="pinyin">Nǐ yéye jīnnián bāshí <em>suì</em> ma？</span><span class="trans">Is your grandpa eighty years old this year?</span>
* 他 女朋友 也 20 <em>岁</em> 吗？<span class="pinyin">Tā nǚpéngyou yě èrshí <em>suì</em> ma?</span><span class="trans">Is his girlfriend also twenty years old?</span>

</div>

Note that you shouldn't use either the verb [[是]] (shì) or the [[measure word]] like [[个]] (gè)  in any of these sentences.

<div class="liju">

<ul>
<li class="x">我 的 孩子 一 <strong>个</strong> <em>岁</em> 。<span class="pinyin">Wǒ de háizi yī <strong>gè</strong> <em>suì</em>.</span></li>
<li class="x">我 的 孩子 <strong>是</strong> 一 <em>岁</em> 。<span class="pinyin">Wǒ de háizi <strong>shì</strong> yī <em>suì</em>.</span></li>
<li class="o">我 的 孩子 一 <em>岁</em> 。<span class="pinyin">Wǒ de háizi yī <em>suì</em>.</span><span class="trans">My child is one year old.</span></li>
</ul>

</div>

== Adding "And a Half" to an Age ==

=== Structure ===

The word for "half" in Chinese is 半 (bàn), and you simply add this after 岁 (suì).

<div class="jiegou">

Subj. + Number + 岁 + 半

</div>

=== Examples ===

<div class="liju">

* 她两岁半。 <span class="pinyin">Tā liǎng <em>suì</em> <strong>bàn</strong>.</span><span class="trans">She is two and a half years old.</span>
* 我 儿子 一 <em>岁</em> 半。<span class="pinyin">Wǒ érzi yī <em>suì</em> <strong>bàn</strong>.</span><span class="trans">My son is one and a half years old.</span>


</div>
